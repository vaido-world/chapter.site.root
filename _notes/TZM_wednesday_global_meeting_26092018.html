GENERAL NOTES(minutes):  
    Short term goals:
        -Education and Awareness activism
        ---about the Economy and Economic Alternatives
        ---about the neccessary value shift and orientation to make the possiblity of a sustainable Economy to emerge.
        ---about Access over Ownership 
        -Advertise the movements, projects, authors, alternatives, videos, etc. that explain the problems of the monetary system and the benefits of a RBE Gift Economy and expose people to the such values with memes. 
        - We should possible consider how our current actions fits in PJ's "Five Great Economic Transitions" https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vLHy7cSdg1U They might be good guidelines for action if we wan't to move forward. (1. Labor for Income to Automation 2. Property to Access 3. Corporations to Open Source 4. Globalization to Localization 5. Price to Networked Digital Feedback)
        - We need to find ways for people to work more hours for the movement
   Long term goals:
        -Bring about the cultural value shift that allows the transition to a sustainable Economy, a Natural Law Resource Based Economy
        -Declare all intellectual and material resources of the Earth as the commonwealth of all life.
