SUMMARY
All of the agenda items were discussed, with most discussion happening around the 'Communication Protocols': The New Human Rights Movement (TZM) Comm Protocol   (Mark Enoch)  https://board.net/p/New_Human_Right_Movement_Comm_Protocol. A suggestion was made that a more accessible verion of the protocols be designed for public release. After the end of the official meeting and the ending of the recording, six or more people stayed for open discussions on many topics related to TZM activism. The open meeting lasted several hours.
