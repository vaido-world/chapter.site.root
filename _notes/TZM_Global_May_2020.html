Michal Mauser: Spoke to many climate activists about Copiosis and RBE but still no interest. Today we got invited to a political party's meeting for creating a program for election. Trying to install an opensource video conferencing browser app called Jitsi for our chapter.

Mark Enoch: I have received 5000 "anti-business" cards, flyers to build a shared world and have started casually handing them out and mail dropping them and putting them on windshields whenever I leave the apartment. 
I have written multiple chain seeds (messages) promoting a shared world and free collaboration networks among the networks that were spontaniously created to help people during the lockdown. This brought dozens of people to join the freecycle group, the resource bank and the car pool group. 
I updated my sharebay account and have been inviting people to it. 
I keep saying "enjoy a shared world" instead of bye to people more and more. I do the "C" hand signal in video calls. I also introduced what I do to a prominent figure in the Rotary district. She is interested to learn more about it. 
I have been reminding people about the monthly global assemblies boths in english and spanish. 
I put on my TZM pin or shirt when I go out. I say "enjoy a shared world" when I pass by people on the trails. 

A spanish TZM forum has been created, https://movimientoz.org/

Fonsi has started holding virtual RBE meetings in spanish in which people well versed in the subject answer the same serious of questions with one minute to answer each question. 

Colin Turner has released sharebay.org
