Gregorio Galati -   we ought to have a deep discussion on what direction the movement is taking, agree on principles and organizational rules. Gregorio Galati - A lateral thought in the same direction:  as an organization we are global, deeply multi cultural and essentially horizontal other institutions we are familiar with (school, business company, political parties) are vertical, local and less diverse which make them easier to organize. As a result  we need a debate to agree on  more effective and formal ways to organize ourselves. I also believe that many of the ups and downs we've been suffering in the last years as a movement along with other problems we see today have root cause in this lack of structure and poor organizational protocols.
