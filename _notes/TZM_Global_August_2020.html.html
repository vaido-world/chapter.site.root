RBE UNITED - an NPO organized for the purpose of creating a workspace for members of the community: to join and collaborate on projects together. This system we're developing

is based on Odoo, a linux-based open source platform, and is design to be a social networking Hub as well as a place for members to collaborate on projects by applying their labor towards projects that are beneficial to the movement. Our hope is to be able to bring together projects such as The Pulse, Koto Coop, copiosis, CubeSpawn and others together under a globally acknowledged "banner" to allow to more streamlined activism to advertise ourselves. 



RBE UNITED is looking for members to join this initiative



- Project managers
- Full stack software developers

- Web developers 

- Content creators (Video/Graphic Design/Music) 


The secondary objective of RBEU is the community project "GARDEN" which is to develop an emergent community within the continental United States predicated on the NLRBE system.
