  1. Changing the focus of TZM to be about content creation
*
As per PJ's email about how the movement needs content creators to be re-invigorated. 

Issue: The movement has been waning. There's very few active members and it likely needs to do something different to survive.

Suggestion: Make The Zeitgeist Movement focused on content creation.

Possible strategy:
  • Change the core focus of the movement.
  • Create a #tzm_media (or #tzm_content_creation ?) Discord chatroom
  • Update the website, Facebook and like to show the change in focus
  • Develop content creation projects. There's already a few on the go  (e.g TZM global and UK website updates, News widget, RBE / TZM short intro videos)
  • We can then start running better tools and systems for content creators. From project management software (Zeitgeist.Earth will likely help with this) to cloud storage of content people can use, e.g TZM Graphics Archive - https://drive.google.com/open?id=0B9y8kbZO5_vvfmlwOHprQllLczZlZmlLMlhXZmV1TUlxQjJIUG1qTnhQMlliblZQcE5pTHM
