Silvering Report & Writtin
        2020-02-29 06:49 (HNE)
        Focus: Introducing my movement concept of education
        

        
        By increasing education of human being, we say no to global slavery,         to exportations of resources, to our wasted resources, to a bad         health system condition, to the boring job that we find useless most         of the time, to replacing human by technologies, to the bad         condition of food that we are eating, to the bad condition of air         that we are breathing.
        We say no to monetary system, we say no to the illusion.
        

        
        I'm starting my own project which I call the UHR : Union of the         Humanitarian Redemption
        A part is the concept of increasing the knowledge of every         individual instead increasing a private corporate system.
        

        
        I want a show by UHR that people can unit their knowledge to be in a         way of self sufficiency by cooperating with the skills that people         own by now or want to earn.
        

        
        UHR is a concept of making people intelligent by being aware of what         they are consuming VS the way they are made.
        By this concept, we keep away the needs of dependence of our big         corporations that are running our societies and thus, the problem we         all facing at the present day.
        

        
        I will call this section ''Step by step'' : What can you teach         today, what can you learn today.
        I'm gonna start to share with you guys simple things that I'm trying         to learn that I have missed from my education of school and I will         encourage you to take 5 minutes each day to tell or just think whats         you have made today that could be useful for people to learn.
        

        
        If you have made your own bread today, share it!
        If you have fixed your car yourself today, share it!
        If you have accomplish something at your work, share it!
        

        
        We need here to understand what it's being hurting us so much and         what brought the situation as it is today
        

        
        Money is knowledge in material. Knowledge replace’s money.
        And here's what a world of money will get us out of:
            - War
            - Poverty
            - Slavery
            - Bad health organization
            - Bad food that are unfit to eat
            - Pollution
            - Immoral animal production
            - Even, you complaining that your car doesn't         start in middle of nowhere with no services and no clue of how to         fix it
        

        
        We will rebuilt this world step by step. All we need is getting out         of the lack of knowledge. Im asking you only 5 minutes to think and         share what you have done and the way you have done it.
